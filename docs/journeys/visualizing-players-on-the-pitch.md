# Visualizing Players on the Pitch

This guide provides instructions on how to view live player positioning and game dynamics on the pitch.

1. **Access the Visualization Screen:**  
   Navigate to the game management area where live pitch visualization is available.

2. **Review Player Positions:**  
   The pitch display will show the current positions of all players in real-time.

3. **Interactive Elements:**  
   Click on player icons to view detailed statistics and playtime information.

4. **Refresh and Update:**  
   The visualization refreshes automatically; if not, manually refresh the screen.

5. **Error Handling:**  
   In case of visualization issues, check for error logs in the console and contact support if needed.
