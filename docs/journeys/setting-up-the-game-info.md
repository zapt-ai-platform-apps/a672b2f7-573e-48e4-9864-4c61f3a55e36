## Important Information

- **Selective Participation**: The app allows you to choose which squad players will play in a particular match, giving you the flexibility to rest certain players.
- **Data Validation**: The app checks for at least one selected player and prompts you if no players are selected.
- **Persistent Data**: Player lists are saved between sessions, but match selections reset each time.
- **Include/Exclude Goalkeeper Playtime**: You have the option to include or exclude the goalkeeper's playtime in the total playtime calculations according to your preference.
