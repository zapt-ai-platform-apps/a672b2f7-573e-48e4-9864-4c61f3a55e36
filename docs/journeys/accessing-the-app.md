# Accessing the App

This guide walks you through signing in and accessing Football Subs.

1. **Open the App:**  
   Launch the Football Subs app in your web browser.

2. **Locate the Sign-In Section:**  
   At the landing page, find the "Sign in with ZAPT" text above the authentication component.

3. **Choose a Social Provider:**  
   Select one of the available social login options (Google, Facebook, or Apple).

4. **Complete Authentication:**  
   Follow the provider's prompts to sign in. The Supabase Auth UI will handle the process with magic link support.

5. **Successful Sign-In:**  
   Once authenticated, you will be redirected to the game setup or dashboard.

6. **Error Handling:**  
   In case of any errors, the app will log details to the console and Sentry for troubleshooting.
