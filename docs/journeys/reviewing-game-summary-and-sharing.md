# Reviewing Game Summary and Sharing

This guide details how to review the game's summary and share results with others.

1. **Navigate to the Summary Screen:**  
   At the end of the game, the summary screen will display final scores and player statistics.

2. **Review Key Metrics:**  
   Examine data such as playtime, substitutions, and performance analytics.

3. **Use the Share Button:**  
   Click on the Share Summary button to generate shareable content.

4. **Configure Sharing Options:**  
   Choose your preferred platform or method to share the game summary.

5. **Confirm Sharing:**  
   Follow any additional prompts to complete the sharing process successfully.

6. **Feedback and Error Handling:**  
   Any sharing errors will be logged, and you can retry if necessary.
